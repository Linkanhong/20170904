What is
QueryTree?

QueryTree is a flexible, powerful, and secure database reporting
and visualization tool that allows people to easily access and
understand their software or app's data.

Implementation takes mere minutes, and enables non-technical team
members to buildand schedule their own reports,pivot tables,
and visualizations wherever and whenever they want to,
without needing the help of a developer.

Implement
Securely connect your MySQL, PostgreSQL, or Microsoft SQL Server database in just a few minutes
Build
Use our intuitive and user-friendly drag-and-drop report builder to explore, select, and sort your data
Visualize
Generate useful interactive charts that visually summarize what's really going on with your software
